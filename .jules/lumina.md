## 2024-05-24 — The Reward of Logic

**What happened:** The user requested to sell the feeling of creating a game, specifically the dopamine hit of the logic working. I designed a 15-second piece where blocks assemble (The Tension) and then connect (The Golden Turn), causing the world to flash and expand (The Revelation).

**The insight:** The dopamine hit of creation isn't just the visual output, it's the moment of connection. When two disparate elements (blocks) finally click together, the brain rewards the resolution of tension. The visual representation must delay this connection, letting the viewer feel the 'almost there' (using magnetic easing), before snapping and releasing the visual energy (screen blend flash).

**Law derived:** The Law of Imminent Connection: When representing logic or creation, the longest easing curve must be applied to the moment *just before* connection. The connection itself must be instantaneous (0 frames), followed immediately by a visual expansion (flash/particle) that decays using a river curve.
**Applied from loop:** Loop 26 (Emotional Calibration)
