## Production Notes

### Equipment Needed:
- Screen recording software (OBS Studio - FREE)
- Microphone (USB mic, $50-100)
- Video editing software (DaVinci Resolve - FREE)
- Webcam (optional, for host segments)

### Recording Tips:
1. Record at 1080p, 60fps for smooth animations
2. Use cursor highlight effect
3. Add zoom-ins for important clicks
4. Keep energy HIGH
5. Speak clearly and slowly

### Editing Tips:
1. Cut out mistakes and pauses
2. Add background music (use our AI Music Generator!)
3. Add text overlays for key points
4. Use transitions between sections
5. Add end screens with subscribe button

### Publishing:
- YouTube (main platform)
- TikTok (short clips)
- Instagram Reels (tips & tricks)
- School LMS platforms
- Discord community
