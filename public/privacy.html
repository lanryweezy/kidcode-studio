Privacy Policy

How we protect your data at KidCode Studio

Last Updated: July 17, 2026
🛡️ COPPA Compliant - Safe for Kids Under 13

1. Introduction

Welcome to KidCode Studio! We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a safe environment for young coders. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. What Data We Collect

KidCode Studio is designed with privacy in mind. Here's what data is involved:

  • Project Data: Your code projects, games, and creative work are stored entirely in your web browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device.
  • Settings: Your preferences (like high contrast mode) are stored locally in your browser.
  • Optional AI Features: If you use AI-powered features (like generating images or getting help), your prompts may be processed by our AI providers. We do not store these conversations.
  • Analytics (Anonymous): We collect anonymous usage statistics (like which blocks are used most) to improve the app. This data contains no personal information.
🔒 Important: We do NOT collect names, emails, addresses, phone numbers, photos, or any personally identifiable information (PII).

3. How We Store Your Data

Your data is stored using two secure methods:

  • IndexedDB: A secure database built into your browser for storing projects and assets.
  • localStorage: Browser storage for settings and preferences.

All data stays on your device. We do not have servers that store your personal data. When you clear your browser data, KidCode Studio data is also removed.

4. No Data Selling

🚫 We never sell your data. KidCode Studio does not sell, trade, or share any user data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. Period.

5. AI Features and Data

When you use AI features:

  • Your prompts are sent to AI providers (like OpenAI) for processing only
  • We do not store your AI conversations
  • AI providers have their own privacy policies
  • Content filtering protects against inappropriate requests
  • Parents can disable AI features entirely in settings

6. COPPA Compliance

KidCode Studio complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We:

  • Do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • Do not require account creation to use the app
  • Store all data locally on the user's device
  • Do not display targeted advertising
  • Do not track users across websites

7. Parental Rights

Parents and guardians have the right to:

  • Review what data is stored on their child's device
  • Delete all data by clearing browser storage or using our data deletion tool
  • Disable AI features in the Settings panel
  • Export their child's project data at any time
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

8. Third-Party Services

We use minimal third-party services:

  • AI Providers: For optional AI features (content moderation applied)
  • Vercel: For hosting the website (no personal data collected)

We do not use analytics trackers, advertising networks, or social media plugins.

9. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures:

  • All data stays on your device (no server storage)
  • HTTPS encryption for all connections
  • Content filtering prevents inappropriate content
  • No account system means no password risks

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We will not reduce your rights under this policy without your explicit consent.
